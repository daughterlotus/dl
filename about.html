Daughter Lotus is the alternative-pop music project of American singer-songwriter Sam Emerizy. Beginning with the single "After You" on August 11th, 2023, he's released 11 singles, three EPs, and three albums independently under his label Stages of Grief Records.

After retiring his previous alias analog broadcast, he released the albums Plasencia and Proof of Concept in late 2023. His EPs January, Driftwood, and Forever, offered his songwriting style combined with an experimentation in different soundscapes. This led up to his latest full-length project Person House, which released on April 11th, 2025.

With the upcoming release of I HATE MUSIC & MUSICIANS on August 30th, Daughter Lotus continues to write, record, and produce his own music.
